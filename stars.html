Stars

The building blocks of galaxies, shining brightly across the universe.

Sun-like Star

A star similar in size and temperature to our Sun.

Red Giant

A dying star in the last stages of stellar evolution.

White Dwarf

A small, dense star that is the remnant of a low-mass star.

Neutron Star

An extremely dense and small star composed mostly of neutrons.

Blue Giant

A very hot and massive star that is much larger than the Sun.

Supernova

The explosive death of a massive star, resulting in a bright burst of radiation.
