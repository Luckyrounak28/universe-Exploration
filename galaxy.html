Galaxies

Explore the vast collections of stars, gas, and dust bound together by gravity.

Milky Way

The Milky Way is our home galaxy, a barred spiral galaxy containing billions of stars, including our Sun.

Andromeda

The Andromeda Galaxy is the closest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way and is on a collision course with our galaxy.

Triangulum

The Triangulum Galaxy is a spiral galaxy approximately 3 million light-years from Earth in the constellation Triangulum.

Whirlpool

The Whirlpool Galaxy is a classic spiral galaxy located about 23 million light-years from Earth in the constellation Canes Venatici.

Sombrero

The Sombrero Galaxy is a spiral galaxy in the constellation Virgo, known for its bright nucleus and large central bulge.

Pinwheel

The Pinwheel Galaxy is a face-on spiral galaxy located 21 million light-years away in the constellation Ursa Major.

Black Eye

The Black Eye Galaxy is a spiral galaxy in the constellation Coma Berenices, known for the dark band of dust in front of its bright nucleus.

Cigar

The Cigar Galaxy is a starburst galaxy about 12 million light-years away in the constellation Ursa Major.

Tadpole

The Tadpole Galaxy is a disrupted barred spiral galaxy located 420 million light-years away in the constellation Draco.
